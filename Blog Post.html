Famous Place in Bihar-Mahaboodth Temple

"Bihar, the land of ancient learning and rich cultural heritage, is home to Buddha's enlightenment and historic landmarks."

Historical Significance

The Mahabodhi Temple marks the location where Gautama Buddha attained enlightenment under the Bodhi tree. It dates back to the 3rd century BCE and remains an important pilgrimage site for Buddhists from around the world.

Architecture and Design

The Mahabodhi Temple is an exquisite example of ancient Indian architecture, built in the traditional Buddhist style.
