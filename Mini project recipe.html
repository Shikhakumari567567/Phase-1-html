Tea Recipe

Nothing beats a cup of hot and refreshing Indian tea

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon black tea leaves
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 small piece of a ginger
  • 1-2 cardamom pods

Instructions

  • Pour water into a pan and bring it to a boil.
  • Let it boil for 2-3 minutes until the color darkens.
  • Add milk and sugar and simmer for another 2 minutes.
  • Once the tea reaches your desired strength, turn off the heat.
  • Strain the tea into a cup and enjoy your perfect homemade chai
